Session 6 · Thursday, 23 April 2026 · 5:00 – 6:00 PM
With Abhijit Jamadagni & Aditya Gore · Abhinav · Hosted by Nihaan Mohammed

The Theme

Five sessions in, the team is shipping. Session 6 is about the craft underneath that — writing code that holds up, keeping design consistent without a designer in every room, and turning the lessons you’ve already learned into skills the rest of the company can use.

Short talks. A workshop. No slides you can’t read. Come with one real correction you’ve had to give AI twice this month — that’s your starting point.

Before You Come

Three small things so the hour lands. None of them takes more than ten minutes.

Pre-session homework

List two corrections. Two times in the last month you had to tell AI the same thing twice. What was it? On what kind of task? That’s the raw material for your skill.
Read your own CLAUDE.md. If you don’t have one yet, write a first draft — the CLAUDE.md template takes five minutes.
Bring your laptop with CLI installed. If Claude Code CLI isn’t installed yet, run the CLI setup prompt before Thursday. The workshop is CLI-first.
