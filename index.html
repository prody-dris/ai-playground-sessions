What Is This?

Every Thursday from 4:00 to 5:00 PM, the product and operations team at Dhwani RIS sits together to learn how to work with AI — not as developers, but as domain experts who happen to have AI as a team member.

These sessions follow two principles:

Richard Feynman: Start from what you know. Use analogies. If it feels complicated, we haven't explained it well enough.

Andrew Ng: Start small. Ship something. Your first attempt doesn't need to be perfect — it needs to exist.

No slides. No theory dumps. Every session, you leave having built something.

Feed it to AI: Paste any session page URL into Claude and say "read this and help me complete the action items."
