How To Use This Site

1

Before a session: Open the latest session page. Read the objective and pre-reading. Come prepared.

2

During a session: Follow along here. All templates and step-by-step guides are on the page.

3

After a session: Come back for reference. Complete the action items. Check the further reading section.

4

Feed it to AI: Paste any session page URL into Claude and say "read this and help me complete the action items."
