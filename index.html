What Is This?

Every Thursday evening, the product and operations team sits together to learn how to work with AI. Not as developers — as domain experts who happen to have AI as a team member.

No slides. No theory dumps. Every session, you leave having built something.

Our approach: Start from what you know. Use simple language. If it feels complicated, we haven't explained it well enough. And the most important thing — just get started. Your first attempt doesn't need to be perfect. It needs to exist. Inspired by Richard Feynman & Andrew Ng

How To Use This Site

1

Before a session: Open the latest session page. Read the objective and pre-reading. Come prepared.

2

During a session: Follow along here. All templates and step-by-step guides are on the page.

3

After a session: Come back for reference. Complete the action items. Check the further reading section.

4

Feed it to AI: Paste any session page URL into Claude and say "read this and help me complete the action items."

Who Is This For?

Product managers, operations leads, domain experts, and anyone at Dhwani RIS who wants to work with AI effectively — without becoming a developer.

You bring the knowledge. AI brings the execution. These sessions teach you how to bridge the gap.
